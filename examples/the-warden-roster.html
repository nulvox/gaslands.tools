THE WARDEN

Warden Cadeila runs the São Paulo People's Penitentiary. Her prisoners are granted a shot at freedom — welded into solid steel "coffin cars" and sent out to race on live television. The deal isn't great for the condemned, but it's better than the alternative.

Why The Warden for Aggressive 50 Cans?

At 50 Cans, most sponsors give you 2 vehicles. The Warden's Prison Car perk gives you 3 — and three aggressive activations per Gear Phase is a massive tactical advantage. You can overwhelm, flank, and dictate pace simultaneously. Access to the Aggression perk class (Battlehammer, Grinderman, Terrifying Lunatic, Murder Tractor) and Badass (Road Warrior, Powder Keg, Crowd Pleaser) gives you perks that punish enemies for being close to you — perfect for an in-your-face style. No other sponsor matches this combination of activation advantage AND aggression-focused perks at this points level.

⚡ Core Gameplan

  • Coffin #1 (Truck, Ram + Battlehammer): Your anvil. Drive it into the thickest fighting. Ram prevents Hazard Tokens from Collisions, and Battlehammer means the Hazard Tokens you do accumulate (from Skid Dice, hazardous manoeuvres etc.) become bonus attack dice on Smash Attacks. The Truck's Crew 3 also lets it fire 3 weapons in an Attack Step.
  • Coffin #2 (Car, Minigun + Grinderman): Your flanker. Grinderman lets you choose to convert Smash Attack hits into Hazard Tokens on the target instead of Hull damage — stacking them toward an inevitable Wipeout without even killing the vehicle. The Minigun's 4D6 at Double Range is also excellent suppressing fire.
  • Coffin #3 (Car, MG): Your wildcard. Cheap, fast (Gear 5), and has one free build slot — see the upgrade options below.

🔥 Coffin #3 Upgrade Options

You have 0 Cans left but 1 free build slot on Coffin #3 and 1 free slot on Coffin #2. Here are optional swap strategies if you adjust the build slightly:

  • Swap Battlehammer (4✦) → Road Warrior (2✦): Saves 2 Cans. Add Mine Dropper (1✦) to Coffin #3 for rear area denial, plus Powder Keg (1✦) to make explosions more dramatic. Budget: exactly 50✦.
  • Swap Grinderman (5✦) → Terrifying Lunatic (5✦): Same cost. Enemies ending their move within Short range of Coffin #2 gain a Hazard Token — passive Hazard pressure without even Colliding.
  • Drop Battlehammer (4✦), add Exploding Ram (3✦, 0 slots) to Coffin #3: Coffin #3 becomes a kamikaze — first front Collision triggers +6 attack dice Smash Attack. Saves 1 Can, which can buy Crowd Pleaser (1✦, Badass) for a free Audience Vote when it wipes out gloriously.

🎲 Hazard Token Management

  • Truck (Handling 2): Roll only 1-2 Skid Dice carefully. You Wipeout at 6 Hazards. Battlehammer actually benefits from accumulating 2-4 tokens before a Smash Attack — but don't push past 5.
  • Cars (Handling 3): More flexibility. Roll 2-3 dice when you need to change Gear or line up shots. Grinderman makes Coffin #2's Collisions devastating so aim for T-Bone impacts at Gear 3+.
  • Ram removes Hazard Token penalty: The Truck's Ram means Collisions don't give it Hazard Tokens on the declared facing. This is critical — you can ram repeatedly without spiralling toward Wipeout from Collision Hazards alone.

⚠ Know Your Weaknesses

  • Low Hull on Prison Cars: 8 Hull on the cars means sustained fire will kill them. Keep them moving and avoid being bracketed.
  • No long-range dominance: All weapons are Double Range, which is good, but you want to close distance. Get in their faces early.
  • Truck's Handling 2: It will fail Skid Dice rolls regularly. Pick straight or gentle templates when you're already at 3+ Hazards. Use the Ram to absorb Collision Hazards without penalty.
  • Audience Votes: You get them when Wrecked, so use them for Thunderous Applause (remove D6 Hazards) or Respawn to stay in the fight.
Rule of Carnage Reminder: When in doubt, choose whatever causes the most carnage. This team is built for exactly that.

🚗 Prison Car Rules

  • Who can take it: Middleweight vehicles only. Once per vehicle.
  • Cost effect: −4 Cans from base cost (minimum 5 Cans total).
  • Hull effect: −2 Hull Value permanently.
  • Build slots required: None (0 slots).
  • Car with Prison Car: 12 − 4 = 8 Cans, Hull 10 − 2 = 8 HP.
  • Truck with Prison Car: 15 − 4 = 11 Cans, Hull 12 − 2 = 10 HP.

💥 Ram Rules

  • Facing: Must declare a facing (front, rear, or side) when purchased.
  • Collision on that facing: +2 attack dice to Smash Attack, AND this vehicle does NOT gain Hazard Tokens from that Collision.
  • Build slots: 1 slot.
  • Cost: 4 Cans.
  • Stacking: A Ram and Exploding Ram on the same facing are cumulative.

🔨 Battlehammer Rules

  • Perk class: Aggression.
  • Cost: 4 Cans.
  • Effect: When making a Smash Attack, this vehicle gains +1 attack die for each Hazard Token it currently holds.
  • Synergy: A Truck with 4 Hazard Tokens making a T-Bone Smash in Gear 3 rolls: 3 (Gear) + 2 (Ram) + 4 (Battlehammer tokens) = 9 attack dice.

🔩 Grinderman Rules

  • Perk class: Aggression.
  • Cost: 5 Cans.
  • Effect: Before rolling Smash Attack dice, this vehicle may choose to add 1 Hazard Token to the target vehicle for each point of damage it inflicts, instead of removing Hull Points.
  • Why it's powerful: Target Wipeouts at 6 Hazard Tokens. Grinderman forces a Wipeout without destroying the vehicle — Wipeout drops them to Gear 1, potentially causes a Flip, and lets an opponent pivot them. Devastating mid-game.

💣 Fireworks (Warden Sponsored)

  • Trigger: When a vehicle in this team Explodes (Explosion check = 6+ on D6 + ammo tokens).
  • Reward: +1 Audience Vote (Middleweight), +2 (Heavyweight).
  • Also: You always get 1 Audience Vote just for being Wrecked (core rule).
  • Explosion check: Roll D6 + number of ammo tokens on the vehicle. 6+ = Explodes, dealing 4D6 Blast to all vehicles within Medium range.

🎯 Smash Attack Quick Reference

  • Head-On: Both vehicles' Gears added together = dice pool.
  • T-Bone: Attacker's own current Gear = dice pool.
  • Tailgate: Faster Gear minus slower Gear = dice pool.
  • +2 dice: For being 1 weight class heavier.
  • −1 die: For being 1 weight class lighter.
  • Hit on: 4+. Critical hit on 6 (counts as 2 hits).
  • Evade: Defender rolls dice equal to current Gear, 6+ cancels 1 hit.
